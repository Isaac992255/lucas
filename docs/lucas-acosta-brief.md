# Lucas Acosta Personal Brand Landing Page

## Project Brief

This document serves as the central reference for the design, copy, and architectural structure of the premium personal-brand landing page for **Lucas Acosta**.

Lucas Acosta is positioned as an expensive, structured, and highly reliable business advisor. He teaches professionals and business owners how to set up robust credit structures, negotiate agreements, and close deals safely.

---

## Identity & Voice

The overall tone is **direct, real, expensive, structured, and reliable**. It intentionally avoids:
- Corporate rigidity (no corporate jargon).
- Generic motivational coaching language ("AI slop").

### Rioplatense Authenticity
All copy is localized to Argentine Rioplatense, written in his authentic, raw style. 

### Forbidden Terms
Due to historical stigma and alignment with premium positioning, never use these terms in public-facing copy:
- `préstamos`
- `prestamista`

Instead, phrase these concepts as:
- `estructuración de capital`
- `financiamiento privado`
- `acuerdos bilaterales`
- `sistemas anti-pérdidas`

---

## Technical Stack

The site is built with modern, performant web technology:
- **Framework:** Next.js 16 (configured for Static Site Export).
- **Core Library:** React 19 + TypeScript.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4.0.0.
- **Components:** Modified shadcn/ui.
- **Typography:**
  - `Sentient` (elegant editorial display serif).
  - `Geist Mono` (structured monospace body).

---

## Section Structure

1. **Hero Section:** Two-column split. Features a high-contrast premium portrait of Lucas paired with the headline *"Hay plata en todos lados. Despiértense."*
2. **Social Proof Strip:** Showcases clean traction metrics (10/10 paid allocations, +500 inquiries/month, 3 years in the business).
3. **Mindset / Value Prop:** Direct problem/solution framing, addressing core friction points (lack of systems, soft empathy, lack of proper legal structure).
4. **Programs / Tiers:** Quad-pricing framework ranging from entry-level products (Ebook, Mentoría Grupal) up to the bespoke Flagship (1:1 Advisory).
5. **My Story:** Narrative arc outlining his path from dependency to managing multi-million-dollar operations.
6. **Footer / Conversion Gate:** Strong final call to action to link directly to his Instagram channel (@lucasramacosta).
